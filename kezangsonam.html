Protecting Nature, Empowering Communities

🌍 The Problem

Our community is facing increasing levels of pollution from plastic waste, vehicle emissions, and improper garbage disposal. This affects our health, environment, and economy.

💡 How It Affects Us

  • 🫁 It harms your body — breathing dirty air can cause coughing, asthma, or serious illness.
  • 😓 It affects your mind — polluted places can make you feel tired, stressed, or sad.
  • 🍽️ It touches what you eat and drink — chemicals in water or soil can get into food and make it unsafe.

🚀 What We Can Do

We can create awareness campaigns, organize clean-up drives, and promote eco-friendly habits.

🏢 About Our Company

GreenBhutan Solutions is a youth-led organization focused on reducing pollution and restoring Bhutan’s natural beauty through education and action.

🎯 Our Goal

To promote sustainable living and environmental awareness in every corner of our community.
