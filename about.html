About Environmental Pollution

Environmental pollution happens when harmful substances like smoke, plastic, chemicals, or waste enter our air, water, and land, making them dirty and unsafe. It affects our health by causing breathing problems, stress, and even making our food and water dangerous to consume

Pollution also hurts animals, plants, and the planet’s balance—leading to climate change and loss of natural beauty.

But we can help by using less plastic, keeping our surroundings clean, planting trees, and encouraging others to take care of the Earth too. Even small actions can create big change.
